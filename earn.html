The Role-Transition Roadmap

Most professionals stay stuck because they never map the jump clearly. This four-step roadmap turns "I want to switch" into a plan you can act on - and teach.

Step 1

Current Role

Be honest about where you are today and the skills you already have.

Step 2

Target Role

Pick the exact role you want next - not vague, but specific.

Step 3

What to Learn

List the skills, tools and certifications that close the gap.

Step 4

How to Prepare

Build a portfolio, mock interviews and content around it.
